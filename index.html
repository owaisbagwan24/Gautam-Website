About US

Since 1932, The Gautam Modi Group has been a trailblazing dealer partner for premier brands, solidifying its position as a leader in Mumbai and Thane regions. With 29 state-of-the-art facilities, we offer a comprehensive portfolio spanning Sales, Aftersales, Insurance, Media Agency and Information Technology. Your trust fuels our commitment, propelling us to invest time and effort in comprehending your needs, fostering relationships based on transparency and mutual understanding. We are dedicated in providing unparalleled service and building lasting partnerships.
